- **10:30 AM-10:45 AM:** Route and target briefing

- **3:00 PM-5:00 PM:** On-ground office visits (only field slot)

**Non-negotiables:**

- No unlogged conversation
- Every interested lead must have email sent and next email date logged

## 6) Office Visit Status Definitions

- **Signed Up:** App installed or listing completed
- **Email Follow-up:** Interested, moved to email sequence
- **No Contact:** Decision-maker unavailable, intro email sent
- **Cold:** Not relevant or not interested now
- **Visit Required:** Physical revisit requested explicitly by the office

**Mandatory contact capture fields per visit:**
- Company name
- Contact person name
- Phone number
- Business email
- Office number and floor
- Status and next action date

**Email-first execution rule:** Follow-up will be done via email. Physical revisit is allowed only when requested by the office.

**Raptor rule:** Every collected business email must be added in Raptor on the same day during closure.

## 9) Risk Controls and Backup Plan

- If regular day coverage drops below 40: add a focused 60-90 minute overflow block next day.
- If Friday full-team coverage drops below 100: run a mini-blitz on Monday with 2-3 extra pairs.
- If conversions are below 20% for 2 consecutive days: run 1-hour script correction workshop same evening.
- If decision-makers are repeatedly unavailable: enforce email-first conversion sequence and schedule visit only on explicit request.
- If technical onboarding issues appear: capture screenshot + send resolution email in same day closure window.

### Failure Prevention Matrix (Must Run Daily)

| Failure Trigger | Early Signal | Immediate Fix (Same Day) | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|
| Daily coverage miss | < 40 offices by 5:00 PM | Add 60-90 minute overflow block next day and rebalance route | Field lead |
| Low conversion | < 20% conversion for 2 days | Run 1-hour script correction and segment-wise pitch reset | Nitin Sir |
| Decision-maker not available | No-contact > 30% of daily visits | Send same-day intro email and move to email sequence | Pair owner |
| Lead leakage | Email follow-up pending > 40 | Run structured email batch before next field slot | Field lead |
| Data loss in tracker | Missing contact fields in > 5 entries | Stop and clean tracker before day close | Coordinator |
| Raptor lag | Emails collected but not uploaded by closure | Mandatory 5:00 PM-5:30 PM Raptor upload block | Coordinator |
| Friday blitz underperformance | Friday < 100 visits | Schedule mini-blitz with 2-3 extra pairs on Monday | Nitin Sir |
| Uneven floor output | One floor consumes > 40% of slot time | Reassign remaining offices to next day's first block | Route planner |

**Hard closure criteria (day cannot close without this):**
- 100% of collected emails uploaded in Raptor
- 100% visit rows with final status
- Next email date fixed for every Email Follow-up and No Contact entry
