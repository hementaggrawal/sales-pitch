Absolutely. Most of our value comes from the layer above the provider: chatbot builder, team inbox, analytics, broadcast scheduling, WhatsApp payments, CTWA routing. What's the difference between WhatsApp Business app and WhatsApp Business API?

The free app is for small solo use — one phone, 256-contact broadcast limit, no chatbot, no analytics, no team inbox. The API (what Jina Connect provides) gives you unlimited broadcasts, chatbot automation, shared team inbox, payment collection, real-time analytics, and CRM integration. Same number, just way more powerful.

I use regular WhatsApp for business. Is that a problem?

Yes — Meta's terms prohibit using personal WhatsApp for commercial messaging. You risk getting banned. The official WhatsApp Business API is Meta's approved channel. Jina Connect gets you verified and protected. Same number, no disruption to your customers.

Can I upgrade from the free app without losing my number?

Yes. Your number stays the same. We port it from the free app to the official API. Your customers won't notice any change — same green bubbles, same chat. But now you have automation, analytics, and a team inbox behind it.
